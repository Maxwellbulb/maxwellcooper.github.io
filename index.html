About

I’m Jase Coop, a digital designer and developer based in London.

I’m available for the design, development and branding of digital products and services.

Working with a variety of companies, from startups to large corporates, my role has seen me act as either a designer embedded within a team, or a small studio delivering the design, build and branding of a website or app.

As a designer and developer, many projects involve me implementing the front-end (and occasionally back-end) code I’ve designed. In some scenarios this might be me designing and building prototypes, in others, designing and building out a custom CMS. This varies from project to project.

My design process is user-centered and I’m a fan of the Jobs To Be Done framework in order to establish and prioritise user, business and techonology objectives.

Contact

For work enquires, please get in touch. I’d be happy to run you more through my process on previous projects and discuss more about how I might fit into your team over coffee.
