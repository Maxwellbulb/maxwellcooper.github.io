About

I’m Jase Coop, a designer and developer based in London.

I’m available for the design, development and branding of digital products, services and brands.

My design process is user-centered and I’m a fan of the Jobs To Be Done framework in order to establish and prioritise user, business and techonology objectives.

As a designer and developer, my role varies from project to project. Many projects involve me implementing the front-end code I’ve designed. In some scenarios this might be me designing and building prototypes. In others, designing and building out a custom CMS. Whereas often I'm solely a designer, collaborating closely with development teams.

Please get in touch for more information on my branding, design and development process.

Contact

For work enquires, please get in touch. I’d be happy to run you more through my process on previous projects and discuss more about how I might fit into your team over coffee.
